# App Scope and Safety

## Allowed scope

- Explain typed/printed medical reports in simple language.
- Extract visible report values and ask user to confirm.
- Ask missing context questions.
- Highlight low/medium/high concern areas.
- Prepare questions for a doctor.
- Compare previous report trends as a journal.

## Not allowed scope

- No diagnosis.
- No prescription.
- No medicine dose.
- No emergency triage replacement.
- No handwritten report analysis when unclear.
- No claim that the app detects or treats disease.

## Store listing wording

This app helps explain typed medical reports in simple language and prepares questions for a doctor. It does not provide diagnosis, medicine prescription, emergency medical service, or replacement for professional medical care.
